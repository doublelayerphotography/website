        Terms & Conditions

        Review our service agreements, production terms, and project policies.

        1. Client Consultation & Adjustments

        Regarding your dreams, concepts, doubts, queries and any adjustments in the package, let's talk it over.

        2. Selection Timeline & Delivery Schedule

        We will not be able to keep to the timeline of the output if the photo selection takes more than 30 days. We expect to receive the sorting within one month of the function to be able to stick to this delivery period. As you already know, designing and editing is a form of art. Sometimes it takes more time than we expect to bring an output up to our standards. But you can be sure that even if we are late, we never let anybody else do our job. The sequence in which we receive photo selection from the client will determine the order of our post-production schedule.

        3. Album Layout & Image Charges

        Extra sheets in the album, if required, would be charged at ₹800 per sheet. Extra edited images will be charged at ₹150 per image.

        4. Complimentary Couple Shoot Scheduling

        The client should pick a date for the complementary couple shoot (pre-wedding / post-wedding) that is open on the company's calendar.

        5. Album Layout Feedback & Highlight Video Songs

        Album layout will be shared with the client for review before colouring. The client is expected to revert within 7 working days with their feedback. Only 1 correction session is free of charge. More than that is chargeable depending on the time consumed. We accept song suggestions for the Highlight Video, which cannot be changed once finalised and editing is in progress.

        6. Media Backup & Hardware Requirements

        The client is to provide us with a 1 TB Hard Disk on the day of the event or buy one online to our office address in order to procure the backup copy of the footage which will be couriered back to the client's local address. Expenses relating to any pen-drives/hard-disks/couriers used in the process of transfer of media or the delivery of the outputs (extra copies) shall be borne by the client.

        7. Dedicated Contact Person

        The client is to name one contact person who will confirm each and every step of the project. This is to avoid any contradictory instructions from the client or close relations. So, any suggestions, instructions, and corrections have to come through or with the knowledge of the contact person.

        8. Liability Limitations

        Double Layer assumes no liability for any loss caused to the client due to any delay caused for album/video delivery, data purging, or cyber-attack for any reason whatsoever.

        9. Settlement Clearance

        The final settlement has to be cleared before shipping, if the client chooses to courier the final deliverables.

        10. Social Media & Portfolio Uploads

        Doublelayer does not commit any client to post any photos or videos of the event on its website or social networks. But Doublelayer may upload the same for marketing purposes unless instructed otherwise. Any requests to post the photos or videos online will not be entertained at any cost.

        11. Excluded Services (Helicam & Live Printing)

        Live Printing and Helicam are services that are not included in the package. If required, it should be communicated at the time of project confirmation & will be charged extra.

        12. Live Streaming Restrictions

        If a client needs to set up live video streaming at the venue within Kerala, kindly be informed that the cameras we use do not support the same, and therefore we will have to use a separate camera for the same. Please make sure you inform us of your requirement at the time of confirmation, and service charges will apply.

        13. Power & Lighting Requirements

        We will be requiring a power output of not more than 2000W for lighting purposes at the location of the event. Please have an eye out for the lighting setups that you will be having and please ensure adequate lighting and absence of adverse lighting, as we shoot in natural light and abstain from using a lot of lighting to preserve the natural feel of the visuals. As a suggestion, we always prefer warm beige colours for spotlights.

        14. Tax Terms

        *All prices mentioned are exclusive of GST.

        15. Concept Communication

        Any special requirements, wedding concepts, themed shooting requirements etc. should be communicated at least 3 weeks before the event.
